Other stories

While my one published book is written with kids in mind, most of the ideas I’m currently working on are geared toward a young adult audience. My mind likes to bounce from story to story and come up with new ideas on a whim, so I have a LOT of works-in-progress at any given moment. I’m also a lot better at starting stories than I am at finishing them, so most of those aren’t especially far along. Because of that and because my writing pace is typically fairly slow overall, I can’t make any promises about when I’ll next finish a story. Hopefully it will be soon, though!

Currently the farthest-along book after “Sunsky” and “Kitty Cat, Supercat” is titled “The Traveler’s Guide to Romance and Wraiths.” When I started it, it grew from one page to around forty quite fast, so that’s a promising sign.
