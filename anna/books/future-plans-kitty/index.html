Kitty Cat, Supercat

Not counting “Sunsky,” “Kitty Cat, Supercat” is my book that's the farthest along. It is a middle grade fantasy novel (with a helping of science fiction elements) starring a kitten named Kitty who is on a quest to become a Supercat. That is no easy feat; the Supercats only accept the best of the best, and joining them means Kitty must leave her current life to travel to a place unlike anything she’s ever known. Kitty has never been one to turn down a challenge, though, and she is more than ready to prove she has what it takes to become a Supercat.

Current state of story: In the middle of major edit to clarify plot.

Likelihood of finishing soon: Define “likely.” And “soon.” If I get back to work editing it (I’ve been working more on other stories lately), there’s a good chance of it not taking too long before the rough draft, at least, is completed.
