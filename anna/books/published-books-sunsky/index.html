Sunsky

The skycats of the island of Aemryl have lived peaceably for centuries with little ever changing. Young council member Sunfur never expected anything to alter that.

Then, disaster strikes Aemryl, forcing the skycats to search for a new land to call home. Sunfur will find herself on a new path, one she never imagined. Can she live up to her responsibility and find a new home for her friends? One thing is for sure: Sunfur is on the adventure of a lifetime, with new lands to be found and new friends to be made.
