About Us

The Traveler’s Guide is the oldest, best-known, and most-respected organization of its kind. We are best known for our primary work The Traveler’s Guide to the World System, which has remained in print consistently for nearly five hundred years and counting. The primary focus of our articles is Travel throughout the System. We may not be the first to report on a breaking news story; but we are renowned for our accuracy and thoroughness, as well as our balanced reporting. The Traveler’s Guide maintains offices throughout the System, with our headquarters being located in Charlane, Ruems.
